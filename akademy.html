Teach what you practice.

The Akademy transmits documentary practice to the next generation of practitioners — through a Fellowship for emerging photographers and an Academy of workshops and masterclasses taught by working documentarians. The knowledge passed here is earned knowledge: from people who have spent years inside communities, not beside them.
