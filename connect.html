CNT · 02 — Academy — Bearing Witness
Masterclass · Zürich · 2024

Seasonal · Public

Masters of the form,
in the room.

Bearing Witness is Artefakt's lecture and masterclass series. Each season we bring a small group of internationally significant documentary practitioners to Calgary and Chicago to teach, exhibit, and speak in public.

The programming is built from Jon's working relationships — thirty years of correspondence, fieldwork, and mutual regard. The 2026–27 season includes Darcy Padilla, Brenda Ann Kenneally, and Kitra Cahana.

CNT · 04 — Bridge Programs — Next Practitioners
Lincoln Challenge · Rantoul IL · 2002

Partners · Schools

The craft gets
passed on.

Artefakt partners with youth-serving organizations in both cities to run bridge programs — short-form, photograph-led workshops for young people who haven't yet named themselves documentarians but are already looking closely.

The Lincoln Challenge partnership is the prototype. More will follow.
