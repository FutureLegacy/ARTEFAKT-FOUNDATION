Pillar 02 / 04 Connect Fellowship · Academy

We teach what we practice.

Connect is Artefakt's Fellowship and Academy. Working documentary photographers and filmmakers teach the craft to the next practitioners — not as theory, but as a working discipline. The Fellowship supports emerging artists with time, mentorship, and a platform. The Academy brings masters of the form to Calgary and Chicago for intensive workshops and public lectures.

The Artefakt Fellowship

Time, mentorship, and a platform.

Each year, Artefakt selects documentary photographers and filmmakers working in Calgary and Chicago — practitioners with a project that requires sustained attention and community trust to realize. Fellows receive a stipend, a year of mentorship with established practitioners, exhibition at Monterey Avenue, and permanent placement in the Artefakt Archive.

The Artefakt Academy

Masters of the form, in the room.

Bearing Witness is Artefakt's lecture and masterclass series. Each season we bring a small group of internationally significant documentary practitioners to Calgary and Chicago to teach, exhibit, and speak in public. The programming is built from Jon's working relationships — thirty years of correspondence, fieldwork, and mutual regard. The 2026–27 season includes Darcy Padilla, Brenda Ann Kenneally, and Kitra Cahana.

CNT · 03 Fieldwork
Field Intensives

The work happens in place.

Connect is not only a classroom. Fellows and Academy participants spend time in the field — on Treaty 7 land in southern Alberta, on Chicago's South Side, along the river corridors and post-industrial edges where documentary practice demands presence. The curriculum follows the work, not the other way around.

CNT · 04 Next Practitioners
Bridge Programs

The craft gets passed on.

Artefakt partners with youth-serving organizations in both cities to run bridge programs — short-form, photograph-led workshops for young people who haven't yet named themselves documentarians but are already looking closely. The Lincoln Challenge partnership is the prototype. More will follow.
