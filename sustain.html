Sustain
Materials Reuse · Funding Model

Nothing of value gets thrown away.

Sustain is Artefakt's funding engine and its clearest philosophical statement. A working materials-reuse and electronics-recycling operation is being established at the gallery at 4525 Monterey Ave NW, Calgary. Revenue funds the Fellowship, the Akademy, and the archive. The logic extends in both directions: nothing of value should be thrown away — materials, memories, communities, or truth.

Sustain · Operations · Materials
Model
Ethical materials-reuse and electronics recycling. Not a charity drop-off — a disciplined operation with triage, restoration, and resale at fair market value through Facebook Marketplace, Kijiji, and Karrot.
High-margin categories
Vintage audio equipment. Designer office furniture (Steelcase, Herman Miller). Dyson motors. Specialty cameras. Laser printers — acquired at near-zero cost, consistent margin.
Sourcing
IT asset disposal companies (Quantum Lifecycle, Calgary). Estate sale acquisitions. Property managers with end-of-lease cleanouts. Referrals from the organizational network.
Revenue
Revenue flows directly to foundation programming — the Fellowship, the Akademy masterclasses, and archive operations. Sustain is the institution's financial spine.
Donate equipment
To donate equipment, discuss consignment, or refer an organization with materials leaving service, contact jon@artefakt.foundation.

Documentary practice taught Jon Lowenstein one thing above everything else: look carefully at what is being discarded. Usually it is not worthless. Usually it just needs someone to pay attention.
