Usage Principles

Ink on Parchment is the default print and screen pairing. It evokes photographic process and archival material — warm, not clinical. The near-black is warmer than pure black, reducing harshness on screen.

Darkroom Red is used sparingly: the f in the wordmark, accent rules, key dates, and calls to action. It refers to the safelight — the one colour that does not expose film.

08 — Usage Rules
Do
  • ✓ Use the wordmark in its established weight (Black 900 or Bold 700) only.
  • ✓ The accent f may be rendered in Darkroom Red on light or dark grounds.
  • ✓ Maintain minimum 16px clear space around the wordmark on all sides.
  • ✓ Use the monochrome wordmark (no accent) when printing in single colour.
Don't
  • ✗ Do not capitalize the wordmark. "ARTEFAKT" and "Artefakt" are incorrect.
  • ✗ Do not stretch, condense, or rotate the wordmark.
  • ✗ Do not place the logo on busy photographic backgrounds without a field.
  • ✗ Do not use digital blue, pure white, or any colour outside the defined palette.
