The Polaroid is the brand's primary metaphor. Not the Instagram filter. The real chemical process — the image developing slowly in your hands, slightly imperfect, bounded by its white border, permanent in a way that digital cannot be. Artefakt makes the same claim about documentary photography: that what is recorded becomes irreversible evidence.

image space
The black field. Holds the light-sensitive surface. Never filled with photography in the mark itself — the void is active.
the letterform
IBM Plex Sans Bold, lowercase 'a'. Always lowercase — Bayer's principle. The capital asserts dominance that documentary practice doesn't earn through hierarchy.
the base border
The Polaroid's chemical band. Wider than the frame border — this asymmetry is load-bearing. It is where the name lives. Where the image is dated and signed.

07 — the four pillars
01
witness
Long-form documentary photography, archive building, the photograph as evidence and argument.
02
connect
Community, platform, and cross-border storytelling between Calgary, Chicago, and beyond.
03
educate
Academy, workshops, fellowships, and public programming rooted in Treaty 7 Territory and South Shore.
04
sustain
E-cycling, materials resale, archive licensing, the reclaimed object as cultural and financial infrastructure.

Sustain is fourth because it is the infrastructure the other three depend on. Innovation will be demonstrated through the work, not announced.

09 — usage rules
do
+
Always lowercase. "artefakt" not "Artefakt" not "ARTEFAKT".
+
The Polaroid mark's base border is always wider than the frame border. This asymmetry is structural.
+
The red f — or the red vertical splice — is the only colour in the wordmark.
+
Asymmetric grid layouts. Centred compositions are not the voice of this organisation.
+
IBM Plex Sans for all primary text. EB Garamond only for body copy and quotations.
+
Hard shadows: 4px 4px 0. Never soft or blurred.
don't
—
Don't capitalize artefakt under any circumstances.
—
Don't add colours beyond the five-colour system.
—
Don't use soft shadows, gradients, rounded corners, or glow.
—
Don't shrink the Polaroid base border — it must always be thicker than the frame border.
—
Don't announce innovation. Show it.
—
Don't use the Instagram-Polaroid aesthetic. The reference is chemical, physical, archival.
