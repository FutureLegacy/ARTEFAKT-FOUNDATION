07 — the four pillars
01
Witness
Long-form documentary photography, archive building, and the photograph as evidence and argument.
02
Connect
Community, platform, and cross-border storytelling between Calgary, Chicago, and beyond.
03
Educate
Academy, workshops, fellowships, and public programming rooted in Treaty 7 Territory and South Shore.
04
Sustain
E-cycling, materials resale, archive licensing, and the reclaimed object as cultural and financial infrastructure.

Sustain is fourth because it is the infrastructure the other three depend on. The recycling operation is not peripheral to the mission — it is how the mission remains possible. Innovation will be demonstrated through the work, not announced.

09 — usage rules
do
+
Always lowercase. "artefakt" not "Artefakt" not "ARTEFAKT".
+
The red f appears in the wordmark on light and dark grounds.
+
Use the contact sheet grid as a compositional device in layouts.
+
The red horizontal bar is the primary structural divider.
+
Asymmetric grids are correct. Centred layouts are not.
+
Box shadow: 6px 6px 0 ink. Hard, not soft. No blur.
don't
—
Don't capitalize the wordmark under any circumstances.
—
Don't add colours outside the five-colour palette.
—
Don't use drop shadows, gradients, or soft glows.
—
Don't place the wordmark on photographic texture without a field.
—
Don't stretch, rotate, or alter letterform proportions.
—
Don't announce innovation. Show it.
