## USA Funding Strategy (Research Complete — April 2026)

Cross-border funding is legal and achievable. Phased approach:

**Phase 1 (Immediate — weeks to set up):**
- Set up **CAF America Friends Fund** (~$3,500 USD/yr fee). Allows US donors to give tax-deductible donations to Artefakt (CA) immediately.
- Obtain **Equivalency Determination** legal opinion (~$1,500–$3,000 one-time) to receive grants from US private foundations.

**Phase 2 (When US fundraising exceeds ~$50K/yr):**
- Incorporate **"Friends of Artefakt USA"** as a 501(c)(3) in the US (4–12 months, ~$5K–$10K).
- Unlocks: NEA funding, major US foundation grants, broader US donor base.

**Key US funders to target (Phase 1+):** Andy Warhol Foundation, Knight Foundation, Bloomberg Philanthropies, Mellon Foundation.
See full list in `Artefakt_Master_Tracker.xlsx` → 🇺🇸 Grants USA tab.
